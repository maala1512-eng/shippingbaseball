Bidding Rules & Protocol

The Shipping World Series is expanding for the 2027 season. Ownership groups must meet the strictest financial and logistical requirements to enter the league.

  • Maritime Connection The ownership group must have a demonstrable connection to the global shipping, maritime, or port logistics industry.
  • Port Facilities The proposed city must possess a deep-water commercial port capable of hosting at least one Panamax-class vessel during gamedays.
  • Financial Solvency Bidders must place a $10M non-refundable earnest money deposit upon submitting formal intent to bid.
  • Labor Agreements The club must adhere to the international dockworkers and semipro-athletes collective bargaining agreement.
